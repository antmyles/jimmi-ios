# Minimal Plus Panel Reference Findings

The supplied reference shows a bottom-sheet style popup with a dimmed backdrop, rounded top corners, a small centered drag handle, and restrained dark surfaces. Its clean feel comes from generous spacing, few borders, no dividers, thin outline icons, left-aligned text, and a monochrome palette with minimal accent use.

For JIMMI, the panel should be simplified further into exactly three functions: Camera, Add Files, and Barcode. It should avoid dense explanatory copy, large nested cards, heavy borders, and competing sections. The preferred treatment is a compact bottom sheet or composer-adjacent sheet with three clear rows or tiles, each using a small icon, concise label, and optional one-line microcopy only if needed for clarity.
